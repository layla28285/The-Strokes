The Strokes

ABOUT

The Strokes are a band from New York City. They were very popular in the early 2000s. However, they were formed in 1998 by Julian Casablancas, Nick Valensi, Albert Hammond Jr, Nikolai Fraiture, and Fabrizio Moretti. The first songs they released were on the EP The Modern Age, and have continued to make music till this day.

The Strokes

Three of the members would play during highschool, showing ther love of music at a young age. Julian Casablancas, Nick Valensi, and Fabrizio Moretti were the original creators of the band. Later, they would add Nikolai Fraiture and Albert Hammond, Jr to their band. They started of small but worked their way up to where they got today.
